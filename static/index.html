Recognition Mode

Goal: Match the target 3D shape to one of the choices below it.

How to play:

  1. Examine the target shape at the top.
  2. Rotate shapes by dragging with your mouse/finger to view from different angles.
  3. Click the "Choose" button below the shape that matches the target.
  4. Get correct answers to increase your score and streak!

Tips: Pay close attention to the number and arrangement of cubes. Some shapes may look similar from certain angles.

Difficulty settings (top toolbar):

  • Memory — controls when the target is visible:
    • Simultaneous — target stays on screen with the choices (easiest).
    • Delayed (5s view) — target disappears after 5 seconds; recall it from memory.
    • Delayed (3s + 2s gap) — target visible 3 s, then a 2 s blank gap before the choices appear (highest working-memory load).
  • Mirror — include a mirror-image distractor (tests chirality / handedness).
  • Parts — include a part-permuted distractor: same pieces, rearranged (tests configural binding).
  • Perspective — render choices from different viewpoints than the target (tests perspective-taking).
  • Expert — larger, more complex shapes (15–25 voxels in a 10×10×10 grid; high topological complexity).

Builder Mode

Goal: Recreate the target 3D structure by adding and removing cubes.

How to play:

  1. Examine the target shape at the top.
  2. Use the colored axes as reference for orientation.
  3. Click faces of existing cubes to add new cubes (in Add mode).
  4. Click cubes to remove them (in Remove mode).
  5. Toggle between Add/Remove modes using the buttons.
  6. Click "Check" to see if your shape matches the target.
  7. Click "Reset" to start over.

Tips: Pay attention to the coordinate system. Your shape must match the target's orientation relative to the axes.
